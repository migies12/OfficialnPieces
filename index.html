The band

About

In Pieces is a hard rock band from Vancouver, BC, whose music strikes a balance between fast and intricate guitar riffs while also creating an ethereal atmosphere for listeners to step into.

They blend heavy and aggressive instrumentals with angelic chord progressions and vulnerable lyrics, inviting listeners to confront the nuances of their own emotions.
